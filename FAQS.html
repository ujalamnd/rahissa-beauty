Frequently Asked Questions

Rahissa Beauty Store - FAQs

1. How can I use the beauty tips provided by Rahissa Beauty?

Our beauty tips are shared on the Blog & Newsletter page. Simply follow the step-by-step instructions for flawless makeup and skincare routines.

2. How can customers buy products?

Customers can shop online via our Shop page or visit our store located in Paramatta Westfield, Floor 3.

3. Where can I check the price of a product?

The prices for all our products are listed on their respective product pages. You can browse by category in the Shop section.

4. How can I search for a specific product?

Use the search bar at the top of the website to quickly find the product you're looking for. Just type in the name or category!

5. Is delivery available?

Yes, we provide delivery services across Australia. Delivery options and charges will be displayed at checkout.

6. What products are suitable during summer?

During summer, we recommend our lightweight foundations, SPF-infused products, and hydrating skincare. Visit our New Arrivals for summer essentials!

7. How can I use the products?

Every product comes with detailed usage instructions on its packaging and product page. For tips and tutorials, visit our Tutorials page.
